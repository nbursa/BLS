CONSEQUENCES OF THE REGISTERED SEAT WHICH IS ASSIGNED THE STATUS OF "FICTITIOUS BUSINESS ADDRESS"

  • If the tax inspection fails to find the company at the address registered in the Serbian Business Registers Agency; or
  • If the financial police is prevented from entering the seat; or
  • If there is no response to phone calls or checks from the local administration; or
  • If delivery of official mail – court summons is rejected; or
  • If the deadline is missed for timely objection or appeal concerning ongoing administrative or court proceedings…
  • …this can result in the unfavorable administrative and tax consequences:
  • The address of the company is declared "inexistent and fictitious";
  • Tax administration revokes the tax identification number ( “PIB” ), all business accounts in banks are blocked and all further operations of the company cease;
  • The court may order a series of decisions against interests of the company: information on the notification board; adoption of the decision due to a missed appeal deadline, etc.
